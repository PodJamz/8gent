# K-Dense Scientific Skills

A comprehensive collection of 139 ready-to-use scientific skills from [K-Dense](https://k-dense.ai).

## Source Repository

Full skill definitions: https://github.com/PodJamz/claude-scientific-skills

## Categories

### Bioinformatics & Genomics
- Sequence analysis, single-cell RNA-seq, gene regulatory networks
- Variant annotation, phylogenetic analysis
- Tools: Scanpy, BioPython, PyDESeq2, gget, AnnData, GGet, ETE Toolkit

### Cheminformatics & Drug Discovery
- Molecular property prediction, virtual screening
- ADMET analysis, molecular docking, lead optimization
- Tools: RDKit, DeepChem, DiffDock, DataMol, MedChem, MolFeat

### Proteomics & Structural Biology
- Protein language models, structure prediction
- Mass spectrometry, peptide identification
- Tools: ESM, AlphaFold Database, MatchMS, PyOpenMS, PDB

### Clinical Research & Precision Medicine
- Clinical trials, pharmacogenomics, drug safety
- Clinical decision support, treatment planning
- Databases: ClinVar, ClinicalTrials.gov, FDA, ClinPGx, DrugBank

### Scientific Databases (28+)
- Literature: OpenAlex, PubMed, bioRxiv
- Chemistry: ChEMBL, PubChem, HMDB
- Genomics: Ensembl, COSMIC, GWAS Catalog
- Pathways: KEGG, Reactome, OpenTargets
- Clinical: ClinVar, ClinicalTrials.gov, FDA

### Data Analysis & Visualization
- Statistical analysis, network analysis
- Publication-quality figures, large-scale data processing
- Tools: Matplotlib, Plotly, NetworkX, Polars, Dask, GeoPandas

### Quantum Computing & Physics
- Quantum circuits, simulations, optimization
- Astronomical data, cosmological calculations
- Tools: Qiskit, PennyLane, Cirq, QuTiP, AstroPy

### Machine Learning & AI
- Deep learning, reinforcement learning
- Time series analysis, model interpretability
- Tools: PyTorch Lightning, PyMC, PyMOO, PufferLib

### Materials Science
- Crystal structure analysis, phase diagrams
- Computational chemistry
- Tools: PyMatGen

### Laboratory Automation
- Liquid handling protocols, equipment control
- Workflow automation, LIMS integration
- Tools: OpenTrons, PyLabRobot

### Scientific Communication
- Literature review, scientific writing, peer review
- Posters (LaTeX, PPTX), slides, schematics
- Citation management, document processing (MarkItDown)

### Research Methodology
- Hypothesis generation, scientific brainstorming
- Critical thinking, exploratory data analysis
- Grant writing, scholar evaluation
